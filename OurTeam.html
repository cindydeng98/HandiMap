About Us


Our Team:
Kiara Cardona(to the right) and Cindy Deng(to the left)


Kiara is a junior and Cindy is a senior in high school. We came up with the idea because we firmly believe this is a problem that needed to be solved. Since we met in Girls Who Code, we were able to bond on common views on this problem. Kiara was so surprised by the lack of accessibility in places. More often then not, she noticed that restaurants and shops did not have ramps. Later Kiara realized that ramps were not the only problem handicap people had to face. With the help of Cindy's technical skills and her passion for this topic, we were able to make this app. We are hoping this app will impact our society and make it easier for handicap people to find accessibility.
